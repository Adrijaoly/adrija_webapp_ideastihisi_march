About Us

About Us

"Making India become technology leader in the area of Cognitive Computing and Social Sensing"

Technology Innovation Hub (TiH) on "Cognitive Computing and Social Sensing" for Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (ICPS), called iHub Anubhuti IIITD Foundation, has been established by Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology Delhi as a Section 8 Company under the aegis of the National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (NM-ICPS) of the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India. iHub Anubhuti is the National Center for Cognitive Computing & Social Sensing. iHub Anubhuti aims at building a collaboration between industries, academia and government agencies on developing data driven cognitive computing solutions, mainly in verticals - Health, Law Enforcement & Security, Education and Environmental Sustainability. It will also encourage and promote the entrepreneurial activities and start-ups emanating from the projects in the areas of “Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and Cognitive Computing & Social Sensing”. The Human Resource Development in the areas of “Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and Cognitive Computing & Social Sensing” is a major goal of iHub Anubhuti. Towards this, it will support manpower at different levels – Undergraduates, Postgraduates, Doctoral, Postdoctoral, Faculty Fellowships & Chair Professors. The aim is to increase the research base of CPS and also support the technical projects that are run under iHub Anubhuti.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

Mission

The mission of iHub Anubhuti is to provide a thriving ecosystem in India for research, development, & entrepreneurship in the area of ‘Cognitive Computing & Social Sensing’ and collaborate with Industries, Ministries and Startups to develop technological solutions which can address short-term and long-term societal needs.

Vision

To establish itself as a hub of Research, Entrepreneurship, and Innovation in the area of Cognitive Computing & Social Sensing.

Build a nationwide shared and distributed Cognitive Computing & Social Sensing facility for public research and commercialization.
