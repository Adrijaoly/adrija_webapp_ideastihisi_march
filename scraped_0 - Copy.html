About Us

Technology Innovation Hub

​An AI & ML Technology Innovation Hub for Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (ICPS) established by the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur under the aegis of the National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (NM-ICPS), DST, Government of India. 


We do fast-paced Technology Translation through Innovation and Start-ups for AI adoption into the traditional CPS and industry. 

Grand Challenge

Fairness, Accountability, Transparency and Explainability (FATE) of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning for Cyber-Physical Systems.
