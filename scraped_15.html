Technology IHub Foundation

Technology Innovation Hub on ​ Intelligent Collaborative System (TIH-ICS) has been established by National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber Physical Systems (NM-ICPS) to create a strong foundation and a seamless ecosystem for Cyber Physical Systems technologies by coordinating and integrating nation wide efforts encompassing knowledge generation, translation research, technology and product development, human resource development, innovation & commercialization standards and international collaborations. TIH-ICS is backed by faculty members with expertise in various aspects of Cyber Physical systems.

IIT Palakkad has established IIT Palakkad Technology IHub Foundation (IPTIF) to facilitate the TIH-ICS. The aims of IPTIF are human resource and skill development, product development, startup incubation, encouraging entrepreneurship and collaboration with other institutions and organizations.
