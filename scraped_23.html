    What we do

    IDEAS Technology Innovation Hub is established by the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, under the aegis of National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (NM-ICPS), Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India.

    We develop solutions to your Data Science or Data Analytics problems.


    Our technologies are backed by strong expertise in Statistics, Data Science, AI & ML.


    You have the data and looking for capable data engineers to curate that? We have the solutions.
